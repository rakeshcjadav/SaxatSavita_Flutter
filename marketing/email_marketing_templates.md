# Email Marketing Campaign Templates

### Email Series Overview
1. **Welcome Series** (3 emails) - New user onboarding

## Email Best Practices

### Design Guidelines
- **Mobile-first:** 60%+ opens on mobile
- **Single column** layout
- **Clear CTA button** (not link text)
- **Alt text** for all images
- **Brand colors:** Match app theme
- **Readable fonts:** 16px+ for body text

### Writing Tips
- **Personal tone:** Use "you" and user's name
- **Short paragraphs:** 2-3 sentences max
- **Bullet points:** For easy scanning
- **Action-oriented:** Tell users what to do
- **Emoji sparingly:** 1-2 per subject, 3-4 per email
- **Value first:** What's in it for them?

### Technical Setup
- **From name:** "Sakshat Savita Team" or "Sakshat Savita"
- **From email:** hello@saxatsavita.com or noreply@saxatsavita.com
- **Reply-to:** support@saxatsavita.com
- **Unsubscribe:** Required, one-click
- **Test:** Send test to multiple email clients

### Timing
- **Best days:** Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- **Best times:** 10 AM or 7 PM (user's local time)
- **Frequency:** Max 2-3 emails per week during launch, then 1 per week
- **Avoid:** Weekends (unless special announcement)

### A/B Testing
Test these elements:
- **Subject lines:** Emoji vs. no emoji, length, personalization
- **Send time:** Morning vs. evening
- **CTA text:** "Try Now" vs. "Get Started" vs. "Explore"
- **Content length:** Short vs. detailed
- **Images:** Hero image vs. no image

### Metrics to Track
- **Open rate:** Target 20-30%
- **Click-through rate:** Target 3-5%
- **Conversion rate:** Target 10-15% (app opens)
- **Unsubscribe rate:** Keep under 0.5%
- **Spam complaints:** Keep under 0.1%

### Segmentation Strategy
1. **New users** (0-7 days)
2. **Active users** (opened app in last 7 days)
3. **At-risk users** (7-14 days inactive)
4. **Lapsed users** (14+ days inactive)
5. **Power users** (daily active)
6. **Engaged readers** (30+ day streak)

### Email Service Providers
Recommended tools:
- **SendGrid** - Good free tier, developer-friendly
- **Mailchimp** - Easy templates, automation
- **Customer.io** - Advanced behavioral triggers
- **Firebase Cloud Messaging** - If using Firebase already

### Compliance
- **CAN-SPAM:** Include unsubscribe, physical address
- **GDPR:** Get explicit consent, allow data export/deletion
- **Privacy:** Link to privacy policy
- **Opt-in:** Double opt-in recommended
