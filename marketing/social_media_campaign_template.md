## Analytics & Tracking

### Key Metrics to Monitor
1. **Download numbers** (daily/weekly)
2. **Social media engagement** (likes, comments, shares)
3. **Click-through rates** on posts
4. **App store conversion** rate
5. **User retention** (1-day, 7-day, 30-day)
6. **Review ratings** and sentiment
7. **Referral sources**
8. **Geographic distribution**

### Tools to Use
- Google Analytics for Firebase
- Facebook/Instagram Insights
- Twitter Analytics
- App Store Connect Analytics
- Google Play Console Statistics
- UTM parameters for tracking campaigns
