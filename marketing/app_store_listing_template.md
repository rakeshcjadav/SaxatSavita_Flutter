## ASO (App Store Optimization) Tips

1. **Update descriptions regularly** with seasonal content
2. **Use all available characters** in descriptions
3. **Include keywords naturally** in your description
4. **Refresh screenshots** quarterly with new features
5. **Respond to all reviews** within 48 hours
6. **Update "What's New"** with every release
7. **Localize** for target markets (Gujarati community worldwide)
8. **A/B test** different screenshots and descriptions
9. **Monitor competitor apps** and adapt strategies
10. **Track conversion rates** and iterate
