## 📊 Success Metrics to Track

### App Store Metrics
- **Downloads**: Total and daily rate
- **Conversion Rate**: Store visits → downloads
- **Rating**: Average star rating
- **Reviews**: Number and sentiment
- **Search Rankings**: For target keywords

### Social Media Metrics
- **Followers**: Growth rate
- **Engagement**: Likes, comments, shares
- **Reach**: Impressions and unique viewers
- **Click-through Rate**: Social → App Store
- **Best Content**: Top-performing posts

### Email Metrics
- **List Growth**: New subscribers
- **Open Rate**: Target 20-30%
- **Click Rate**: Target 3-5%
- **Conversion**: Email → App Opens
- **Unsubscribe Rate**: Keep under 0.5%

### App Metrics (via Firebase/Analytics)
- **Active Users**: DAU, WAU, MAU
- **Retention**: 1-day, 7-day, 30-day
- **Session Length**: Average time in app
- **Feature Usage**: Which features are used
- **Reading Streaks**: Habit formation indicator

### Business Metrics
- **Cost Per Install (CPI)**: Ad spend / downloads
- **Lifetime Value (LTV)**: If monetizing
- **Organic vs Paid**: Download attribution
- **Viral Coefficient**: Referrals per user
- **Time to Milestone**: Days to reach goals

## 🚨 Common Pitfalls to Avoid

### ❌ Don't Do This:
1. **Spam communities** with promotional links
2. **Ignore negative feedback** - address it constructively
3. **Buy followers** - fake engagement hurts you
4. **Inconsistent posting** - algorithm punishes gaps
5. **Copy competitors** - be authentic and unique
6. **Neglect email list** - it's your most valuable asset
7. **Forget to track** - data drives decisions
8. **Burn out** - pace yourself for long game

### ✅ Do This Instead:
1. **Add value first** in communities, promote second
2. **Engage authentically** with all feedback
3. **Grow organically** with real, engaged users
4. **Post consistently** even if less frequently
5. **Find your unique voice** and lean into it
6. **Nurture email subscribers** with great content
7. **Review metrics weekly** and adjust
8. **Build sustainable routines** you can maintain
